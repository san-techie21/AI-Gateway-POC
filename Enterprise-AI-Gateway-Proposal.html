Enterprise AI Gateway

Enabling Secure, Compliant AI Access for BFSI Organizations Without Compromising Innovation

February 6, 2026 | Version 2.0

Executive Summary

The financial services industry stands at a critical juncture. Business teams demand AI-powered tools for research, strategy validation, and competitive analysis. Meanwhile, security and compliance teams rightfully point to SEBI's AI/ML Guidelines (June 2025) and RBI's data localization requirements that make unrestricted AI access a regulatory minefield.

The Core Challenge

Business Reality: Teams across research, strategy, and operations want to leverage AI for market analysis, competitor research, and strategy validation. They see competitors using ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI tools to gain advantages.

Compliance Reality: Sending sensitive financial data, UPSI (Unpublished Price Sensitive Information), or client information to external AI providers violates SEBI PIT Regulations 2015, SEBI AI/ML Guidelines 2025, and potentially RBI data localization rules. Violations can result in penalties up to Rs. 25 crore or imprisonment.

Result: Internal conflict between innovation-seeking management and risk-aware security teams, with no clear path forward.

The AI Gateway Solution

An intelligent middleware layer that enables both goals simultaneously:

  • Research Mode: Full access to 40+ AI models including GPT-5.2, Claude Opus 4.5, Gemini 3 Pro, and Perplexity for general research, market trends, and public information analysis
  • Secure Mode: Automatic detection and routing of sensitive content to on-premise LLMs where data never leaves the corporate network
  • Compliance Built-In: Real-time scanning for PAN, Aadhaar, UPSI patterns, client names, and proprietary trading strategies before any data reaches external APIs

Result: Business teams get the AI access they need. Security teams get the compliance they require. Everyone wins.

Key Regulatory Update: SEBI AI/ML Guidelines (Effective Q4 2025)

SEBI's consultation paper from June 2025, now in implementation, requires:

  • Human oversight of all AI/ML deployments affecting customers
  • Disclosure of AI usage to clients in simple language
  • Testing in segregated environments before production
  • 5-year retention of input/output data for audit
  • Periodic accuracy reporting to SEBI

The AI Gateway provides all these capabilities out of the box.
